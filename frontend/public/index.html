By Service

Fragmented domains (CDNs, gateways, APIs) grouped under their parent app. Estimated active time is derived from DNS lookup clustering on the busiest domain in each group — it undercounts due to caching and is best read as a relative indicator, not a precise measurement.

Insights

Deeper patterns mined from the raw logs. Spikes and co-occurrence are reliable; overnight activity is a strong background-traffic indicator. All are analytical inferences, not certainties.

Detected Apps

Apps inferred present on the device from their DNS signatures, with likely-active usage sessions. This is an inference from traffic patterns, not a phone usage log — caching hides some use, and umbrella platforms can't be pinned to exact opens. Transactional apps (banking) may show 0 content sessions despite being used.
